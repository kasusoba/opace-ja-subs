What everything does

The line list (right) — every official line in order.
  • blue time = shown on screen; click the time or text to seek there. The playing line highlights and auto-scrolls (toggle follow).
  • grey, no time = unplaced (not shown). When one falls in the gap you're watching it turns amber (in gap) — a "is this spoken here?" candidate.
  • method badge: pin human-placed · interp inferred (be skeptical) · rescue/part fuzzy · cover heard.
  • click the #number (or double-click a row) to open its flag buttons.
  • Ctrl/⌘-click or Shift-click rows to multi-select, then: Place block @ playhead (lay unplaced lines out from the playhead by official spacing), Remove all, or right-click the correct line to mark the selected ones wrong. Esc clears.
  • Time a line fast: click it to focus (accent outline), then mark its spoken start and end — by keys i/o or the ⇤ in / out ⇥ buttons under the video. Frame-accurate, and it updates one note in place — no pile-up of retimes. Works on an unplaced line too (placing it).
Flag buttons — per row, or the bar under the video for the current line. Each flag previews on the track live (the line moves/changes/appears/vanishes as you flag), but the real .ass is baked by the pipeline from your exported notes — so nothing is silently changed.
  • ✕ remove — shown but shouldn't be (One Pace cut it).
  • ⚑ here — an unplaced line is spoken now (records the time).
  • ⏱ retime — right line, wrong time (e.g. One Pace trimmed it): drag the start/end tips on the slider (the video scrubs as you drag) or type a new start/end (blank = keep).
  • ✂ trim — One Pace cut part of the line: click a character to set the nearest edge (or drag the tips / ◀▶) and keep only the spoken substring of the official text — never typed/edited.
  • ≠ wrong — wrong line shown; type what it should be.
  • ✏ fix text — the official line text itself is wrong; type the corrected text (timing stays). Differs from wrong, which swaps in a different official line.
  • ✎ note — anything else, tied to the moment.
  • ▭ no sub here (bar) — a whole stretch (from→to) has no subtitle.
Keys (rebind/disable in ⚙) — space play/pause · ←/→ previous/next subtitle · a/d seek back/forward by the interval (default 3s) · ,/. 0.1s nudge · x remove current · c note now · i/o time the focused line (mark in / out). Subtitles are selectable — hover with Yomitan to look up words.
